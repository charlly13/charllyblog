## Email Security Fundamentals

### Protocols

- SMTP: core protocol for sending mail.
- DNS & MX records: direct mail delivery to correct servers.

### Core Principles

- Confidentiality, Integrity, Authentication, Non-repudiation.

### Authentication & Anti-spoofing

- SPF: authorizes sending IPs for a domain.
- DKIM: cryptographic signatures in headers.
- DMARC: policy for handling SPF/DKIM failures.
